Kymberly Westall


My name is Kymberly Westall and I am a visual artist, designer, community artist & Learning disabilities art instructor working throughout East Lancashire.

My passion for empowering others to make art is the driving force behind my practice. Encouraging someone's development to explore their creativity and learn new skills are the primary objectives of my work. Seeing someones confidence increasing, witnessing benefits to their wellbeing and the pride they hold in their makings are the reasons I focus my practice on inclusivity, accessibility and social inclusion in the local community.
